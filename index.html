This is not your mother's "dinner theater"

In Foul Play, put your sleuthing skills to the test as you participate in fully interactive murder mystery games. Embrace your character, crack clues, dabble in deceit, and complete your own agenda in a game where the end always justifies the means. You decide what path to take to fulfill your goals and solve the murder. However, not everyone is as they seem, and enemies can be made just as easily as friends.

Establish your innocence, condemn the guilty, and get ready for some Foul Play.

Our Parties

At Foul Play, YOU live the mystery. We provide a full set of characters, the plot, props, and inventory. You choose the venue, and invite guests to come in costumes for a fully personalized murder mystery experience!

From 1920's gangsters to the Wild West and everything in between, we cover a wide variety of scenarios each with the full Foul Play experience. We even have a Harry Potter mystery, for a glimpse into the dark underbelly of the wizarding world at Hogwarts.

Perfect for Every Occasion

Need to throw an epic holiday party? Hosting a team building activity? Or just want to get dressed up with your friends? At Foul Play, we tailor our scenarios to your event, and make sure everyone leaves with an unforgettable evening.
